Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Chroblox collects, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Chroblox does not require user accounts or registration. When you use our service, we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (pages visited, session duration) via Umami Analytics.
  • Browser-stored preferences (theme selection, sidebar state) via localStorage — this data never leaves your device.
  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) processed by our hosting provider.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Understand how users interact with the site and improve the experience.
  • Monitor server performance and prevent abuse.
  • Display relevant advertisements through our ad partners.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Umami Analytics — privacy-focused analytics that does not use cookies or track personal data.
  • Adsterra — our advertising partner, which may use cookies to serve ads. Please refer to their privacy policy for details.
  • Cloudflare — DNS and security services that may process connection data.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Chroblox itself does not set tracking cookies. However, our advertising partners may use cookies to serve and measure ads. You can disable third-party cookies through your browser settings.

We use localStorage (browser-side storage) to save your preferences such as theme choice and sidebar state. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Proxy and Browsing Data

Chroblox provides a web proxy service. Pages you visit through the proxy are routed through our server but we do not log, store, or monitor the content of your proxied browsing sessions.

6. Data Retention

Aggregated analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes, then automatically deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us for removal.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal accounts or identifiable data, there is generally no personal data to access or delete. If you have concerns about any data associated with your usage, contact us and we will assist you.

9. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via the site. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at Contact.Chroblox@gmail.com
